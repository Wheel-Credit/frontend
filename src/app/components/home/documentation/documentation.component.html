Smart Vehicle Purchase Solution

Welcome to our web application designed to provide an intelligent experience in the vehicle purchasing process through the innovative French amortization method. This tool has been created to meet the needs of the financial and banking environment in Peru, offering a payment plan tailored to each user and authorized financial institution.

"Our Smart Purchase" not only performs precise financial calculations but also allows users to configure key aspects such as grace periods and interest rates, ensuring a comprehensive and personalized solution. Additionally, the application guarantees data security through an authentication system with valid credentials.

From the perspective of financial institutions, a comprehensive database is maintained, including detailed information about customers and vehicle offers. We comply with the regulatory standards of the Peruvian Financial System, ensuring transparency and regulatory compliance.

Key Actions

This feature allows you to initiate a new credit plan by filling out a comprehensive form. Provide essential details such as the selling price of the asset, desired payment plan type, and other relevant information. Once the form is completed, the data will be securely saved in the database, enabling seamless processing for your customized credit plan.

Explore your credit journey effortlessly with this feature. Access a detailed history of your previous credit plans, including payment structures, interest rates, and other pertinent information. Stay informed about your financial decisions and track the progress of each credit plan you've created, ensuring transparency and easy management of your financial history.
